About Tuwaze

Tuwaze (Swahili for "let's enlighten") is a civic engagement and community safety platform designed specifically for the residents of Eldoret, Uasin Gishu County.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and local government, promote transparency, and improve access to justice information through technology.

The platform is built with privacy and security in mind, offering both identified and anonymous participation options to suit different needs.
